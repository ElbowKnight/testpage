**AIESEC** is a non-governmental and non-profit organisation led by students and youth, that provides business development, volunteering and internship opportunities, with the target of creating social impact.
## Network
AIESEC has over 40 000 members, and is established in over 120 countries. 
## Name origin
The name comes from the French: Internationale des étudiants en sciences économiques et commerciales (International Association of Students in Economics and Business).
