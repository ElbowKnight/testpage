A department is a section of a committee, where one or several similar areas are managed. Each department should have, at least, a [[Vice President - VP|Vice President]] to work, and additionally, it may have [[Team Leader - TL| team leaders]] and [[Team Member - TM|team members]] for support of the tasks needed to be fulfilled. Most of the time, the main four departments in most Local Committees are: [[Department of Product - oGX]], [[Member Experience - MX]], [[Finance and Legal Department - F&L]], and [[Department of Marketing - MKT]].
