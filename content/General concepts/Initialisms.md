In [[AIESEC]], it is common to refer to many parts of the organisation with initials instead of the whole name. At first, it may be confusing, although once experience may be gained, they become the day-to-day language.
## Most common initials
### General roles
- Local Committee: **LC**
- Member Committee (national): **MC**
- Executive Board: **EB**
- Local Committee President: **LCP**
- Member Committee President (national): **MCP**
- Vice President: **VP**
- Team member: **TM**
- Team leader (manager): **TL**
### Departments
- Outgoing global volunteer: **OGX**
	- Incoming global volunteer: **iGV**
	- Outgoing global talent: **oGT**
	- Incoming global talent: **iGT**
	- Outgoing global entrepeneur: **oGE**
	- Incoming global entrepeneur: **iGE**
- Member experience (human resources included): **MX**
- Marketing: **MKT**
- Finance and Legalities: **F&L**
- Business development: **BD**
