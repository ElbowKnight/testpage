Each Local Committee needs to fulfil a series of tasks in order to be recognised as a member of AIESEC. Below these tasks are listed, sorted by responsibility and area.
## Local Committee President (LCP) tasks
### Representation criteria
- **Representation at the meeting of BoP:** The president of each entity will be required throughout the duration of it (both ordinary and extraordinary assemblies, either physical or virtual).
- **Composition of the board of directors:** It must include the following, included in a document with full names, position and emails that must be submitted. ^b23fe9
	- Local Committee President (LCP)
	- Vice President of Finance and Legal (VP F&L)
	- Vice President of Human Resources (VP MX)
	- Vice President of Marketing (VP MKT)
	- At least one Vice President of Product (oGX). In case the LC has several outgoing products, it must have at least as many VPs of product as the MC establishes. The same rule applies if the LC has one or more incoming products.
### Information criteria
- **Infrastructure:** Certificate signed and stamped by the Dean's Office or the Student Delegation of their respective university (or faculty) stating that the committee is recognised by the university and that it is active according to its statutes. If required by the MC, it must be sent physically.
### Fiancial criteria
- **Electronic Certificate / Bank transactions (with VP F&L):** Valid electronic certificate in the name of the association in order to be able to carry out the obligatory electronic procedures (VAT, IS, Third Party Declaration, etc). It must be sent annually to the MCVP F&L together with the keys and expiry date of the certificate.

## Executive Board (EB) tasks
### Representation criteria
- **Full board participation in conferences:** Full board participation per entity will be required on all days of the conference.
- **Touchpoints attendance:** Presidents must attend 80% of these, vice presidents must attend 50% of these.
### Information criteria
- **Month SONA:** It must be sent with the template sent by the MC on the $1^{st}$ of the first month of the following month. It will include questions that cover network questions and can be measured. SONAs sent at the end of each quarter must include network questions and additional questions where we measure operational numbers.
- **Planning and budget:** Documents sent by the MC must be completed and submitted. Deadlines will be January $31^{st}$ and July $31^{st}$ respectively, this deadline may be extended under permission of the MC for special situations. Failure to do so will result in the loss of voting rights for the rest of the semester corresponding to the semester of submission of these criteria.

## Vice President of Finance and Legal (VP F&L) tasks
### Information criteria
- **Finance SONA:** A biweekly **State of National Association** that needs to be filled by the $15^{th}$ and $1^{st}$ day of every month at 23:59 hrs (GMT+1). This criteria will be established by the finance and legal department head. For the local entity get the criteria it needs to have all the information by the end of the month as maximum.
- **GFB Financial consolidated report:** It must be completed and submitted based on the global template sent by the MC by 23:59h on the $3^{rd}$ of each month. This report will count as the **Balance Sheet and Budget submission** for the particular month.
### Financial criteria
- **Local accounting:** For an invoice to be considered valid it will be necessary to have the following information: name of the receiver, address, identification document, date, concept, amount to be paid and the invoice number.
	- Balance sheet (annual)
	- Profit and loss account (annual)
	- Trial balance (quarterly)
	- Journal (quarterly)
	- General ledger (quarterly)
	- Book of invoices issued and received (quarterly)
	- Book of corrective invoices issued and received (quarterly)
	- Invoices issued and received (quarterly)
	- Corrective invoices issued and received (quarterly)
	- Voucher for each bank movement: invoice, ticket, budget (quarterly)
- **Electronic Certificate / Bank transactions (with LCP):** Valid electronic certificate in the name of the association in order to be able to carry out the obligatory electronic procedures (VAT, IS, Third Party Declaration, etc). It must be sent annually to the MCVP F&L together with the keys and expiry date of the certificate.
